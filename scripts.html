MOTHER

MOTHER

What?

The mother starts to flush, completely caught off guard.

MOTHER

Well I can’t do it. It's my most liked photo.

The kid grabs his coffee-to-go mug and paper bag’d sandwich.

CHILD

I don’t know, Mom, if you like it so much, put it on the fridge instead; but if you won't comply, we can let the lawyers hash it out. Expect a cease and desist in the morning.
